# 💀 Pathfinder 2e: The Void Shepherd — Ultimate Zombie Swarm Necromancer

> **Concept:** A Wizard (Necromancy School) / Undead Master who maximizes the number, durability, and lethality of undead minions while weaponizing negative/void energy as both offense and sustain. This build leverages the **Undead Master** archetype, **Staff of Necromancy**, and the **Void** tradition to field the largest, deadliest zombie horde possible.

## Ancestry & Heritage

### Dhampir (Versatile Heritage)
- **Blood Draining (Dhampir):** Heal yourself by draining blood from undead — synergizes with your own horde
- **Negative Healing:** You are healed by negative energy (void), not harmed. Your undead minions won't drain you with friendly fire
- **Void Resistance:** Resistance to void damage equal to half your level
- **Gnaw Bones (Ancestry Feat 1):** Unarmed attack that deals negative damage — useful early

### Ancestry Feats Progression
| Level | Feat | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Dhampir Clan Abilities** (Moroi-Born: Svetocher) | +1 to saves vs. magic; status bonus to Deception vs. living creatures |
| 5 | **Blood Drain** | Heal yourself through your undead, synergy engine |
| 9 | **Undead Slayer** *(optional swap)* | Bonus damage vs. living to maintain flavor or take Vampire Dedication |
| 13 | **Vampire Dedication** *(Dhampir Lineage path)* | Unlocks vampire-adjacent abilities; negative healing amplification |

## Class: Wizard — School of Necromancy

### Why Necromancy School?
- **Drain Bonded Item** refunds a spell slot once per day for necromancy spells
- **Necromancy School Spells:** Access to **Call of the Grave** (school cantrip: 1 undead, 30 ft range, void damage + clumsy)
- **Spell Slots:** Full spellcaster progression — critical for summoning multiple **Animate Dead** spells simultaneously
- **Thesis:** **Spell Substitution Thesis** — swap out prepared spells during the day when you need more summon slots

## Level-by-Level Build

---

### ⚰️ Level 1

**Class Feats:**
- **Familiar** — Use your familiar as a scout, spell component courier, and battery for cantrips. Choose a Bat familiar for darkvision and flying recon.

**Skills:** Arcana (T), Occultism (T), Religion (T), Medicine (T), Intimidation (T)

**Spells Prepared:**
- Cantrips: **Chill Touch** (void, 1d4+INT void damage, enfeebled 1 on crit), **Daze**, **Prestidigitation**, **Detect Magic**, **Call of the Grave** (school cantrip)
- 1st Rank: **Animate Dead** ×2, **Fear**, **Ray of Enfeeblement**

**Key Notes:** **Animate Dead** at rank 1 produces a **Zombie Shambler** (2 HP = level, Strikes deal 1d6+4 bludgeoning). You can maintain **2 minion spells** simultaneously (capped by your 2 actions to Command). Keep both zombies adjacent to flank with each other.

**Equipment:**
- **Spellbook** (standard issue)
- **Staff of Necromancy (Lesser)** — +1 charges per day for necromancy spells if you cast from the staff
- **Component Pouch**
- **Studded Leather Armor** (Mage Armor prepared as alternative)
- **Wand of Animate Dead (1st Rank)** — extra summon per day

---

### ⚰️ Level 2

**Class Feat:**
- **Reach Spell (Metamagic)** — Extend Chill Touch and other touch spells to 30 ft. range. Also works on **Harm** when you take it.

**Spells Prepared:**
- Add: **Mage Armor**, **Grim Tendrils** (1d4 void damage in a 30-ft line, persistent void damage on crit, affects multiple targets)

**Key Notes:** **Grim Tendrils** is your first AOE void damage spell. On a critical hit it deals persistent void damage — excellent for chip damage between zombie activations.

---

### ⚰️ Level 3

**Ancestry Feat (Dhampir):** None yet / take a General Feat

**General Feat:** **Incredible Initiative** — Go first, set up your horde before enemies act.

**Spells Prepared (Rank 2):**
- **Animate Dead** (rank 2) — Now summons a **Zombie Hulk** (faster, harder hitting) or 2 Zombie Shamblers as one spell
- **Vampiric Touch** — Touch for 2d6 void damage, gain HP equal to half; your Dhampir Negative Healing means you can sustain yourself this way
- **False Life** — Temporary HP buffer; stacks with your horde's meat-shielding

**Skill Increase:** Religion → Expert

---

### ⚰️ Level 4

**Class Feat:**
- **Bespell Weapon** — After casting a necromancy spell, your next Strike deals +1d6 void damage. Works with your Chill Touch cantrip chain.

**Spells:** Add **Web of Eyes** (scouting utility with undead familiar chain)

**Equipment Upgrade:**
- **+1 Striking Handwraps of Mighty Blows** *(if using touch attacks)*
- **Skeleton Key Amulet** *(grants +1 to Occultism checks to control undead)*

---

### ⚰️ Level 5

**Ancestry Feat:** **Blood Drain** — You can drain blood from an adjacent living or undead creature as an action. Heal 1d6 HP per two levels. This is your emergency panic button.

**Skill Increase:** Arcana → Expert

**Spells Prepared (Rank 3):**
- **Animate Dead** (rank 3) — Summons a **Zombie Brute** or **Skeletal Champion** (AC 20, HP 60+)
- **Vampiric Maiden** — Single-target void damage + drain, refuels your HP
- **Bind Undead** — Permanently bind a pre-existing undead to your service without concentration (frees up spell slots)

**Archetype Entry:**
- Take **Undead Master Archetype Dedication** — This is the backbone of the build

**Archetype Feat:** **Undead Master Dedication**
- You can control undead up to your level in total HD of undead simultaneously (instead of the normal spell cap). This is the critical unlock — your **total zombie headcount skyrockets**.
- You gain **Command Undead** as a free action 1/round when you use Command for a minion you summoned.

---

### ⚰️ Level 6

**Class Feat:**
- **Spell Penetration** — Reduce resistance to your spells by 5. Void-immune creatures are a threat; this helps pierce partial resistances.

**Archetype Feat:** **Animate Minion** *(Undead Master)*
- 10-minute ritual to animate a corpse as a permanent undead servant (not requiring a spell slot). The undead has your level as its base level. This is free standing zombie creation — limited to 1 at a time without further feats, but the key to off-combat horde building.

**Spells:** Add **Spiritual Weapon** (free action bonus attacks) and **Enervation** (rank 3, multiple void damage rays)

---

### ⚰️ Level 7

**General Feat:** **Toughness** — You need to survive long enough to command your horde. +Level HP, reduced dying condition.

**Spells Prepared (Rank 4):**
- **Animate Dead** (rank 4) — Summons a **Zombie Lord** or two Zombie Brutes
- **Enervation** — Four rays of void damage, each dealing 2d8; target loses a 1st or 2nd rank spell slot on a critical hit
- **Vampiric Exsanguination** — 30-ft cone of void damage (4d6), you gain HP equal to half

**Skill Increase:** Intimidation → Expert (for Demoralize support)

---

### ⚰️ Level 8

**Class Feat:**
- **Spell Combination** — Combine two spells into a single action when both have the same spell rank or lower. Lets you cast **Animate Dead + Chill Touch** as one action occasionally.

**Archetype Feat:** **Rotting Presence** *(Undead Master)*
- Enemies that start their turn adjacent to your undead minions take 1d6 void damage (no save). With 4-6 zombies, every square adjacent to your horde becomes a void damage hazard.

**Equipment:**
- **Staff of Necromancy (Major)** — Upgrade. Provides significant spell charges for Animate Dead and Harm daily. Essential.
- **Bracers of Missile Deflection** — Survivability upgrade

---

### ⚰️ Level 9

**Ancestry Feat:** **Vampire Dedication** *(requires Dhampir)*
- You gain the **Vampire** versatile heritage feats path
- Resistance to void damage = your level
- You can feed on blood to gain temporary HP
- **Harm** heals you the same as it heals undead

**Spells Prepared (Rank 5):**
- **Animate Dead** (rank 5) — Summons **Skeletal Titan** or **Zombie Hulk Lieutenant** (AC 27, ~120 HP)
- **Cloudkill** *(splash utility for area denial)*
- **Synesthesia** *(debuff)*
- **Void Body** — You become incorporeal for 1 round; void damage you deal is maximized for that turn

**Skill Increase:** Religion → Master

---

### ⚰️ Level 10

**Class Feat:**
- **Spell Tinker** — Modify spells on the fly. Particularly useful for adjusting **Animate Dead** to fit available corpse types.

**Archetype Feat:** **Command the Horde** *(Undead Master)*
- You can use a single **Command Undead** action to give the same order to ALL undead minions simultaneously. This is the single most important combat feat in the build. Previously you could only command 1-2 per turn; now your entire zombie army acts on your one action.

**Equipment:**
- **Ring of Wizardry (Rank 3)** — Extra 3rd-rank spell slot = extra Animate Dead per day
- **Cloak of the Bat** — Fly speed + darkvision; you need mobility to command your spread-out horde

---

### ⚰️ Level 11

**General Feat:** **Ancestral Paragon** — Gain an extra 1st-level ancestry feat. Take **Gnaw Bones** or **Drain Bonded Item** utility.

**Spells Prepared (Rank 6):**
- **Animate Dead** (rank 6) — Summons **Greater Death Knight** (AC 30+, 180 HP, sword + shield)
- **Phantasmal Calamity** — Massive void-flavored psychic AoE + fear combo
- **Field of Razors** — Zone that deals bleed damage to living enemies crossing it; your undead are immune, so they march through freely
- **Harm** (rank 6) — 6d8+spellcasting mod void damage in a 30-ft emanation, heals all your undead simultaneously

**Skill Increase:** Occultism → Master

---

### ⚰️ Level 12

**Class Feat:**
- **Quickened Casting** — Once per day, cast a rank ≤ (highest rank -3) spell as 1 action. Use for a free **Animate Dead** at rank 3 or below.

**Archetype Feat:** **Army of the Dead** *(Undead Master)*
- Your **Animate Minion** ritual can now create up to 4 permanent undead simultaneously (limited to your level in total HD)
- Between combats you can field a genuine army — 4-8 zombies depending on their HD

**Equipment:**
- **Wand of Manifold Missiles** *(backup ranged option)*
- **Phylactery of Positive Channeling** *(reserve — you can use Harm to heal your undead in a 30-ft burst)*
- Upgrade armor to **+2 Resilient Studded Leather**

---

### ⚰️ Level 13

**Ancestry Feat:** **Drain Blood (Vampire)** — Your blood drain now restores full level in HP and can be done through your undead conduit within 15 ft. Full necromantic energy cycling.

**Spells Prepared (Rank 7):**
- **Animate Dead** (rank 7) — Summons **Dullahan** or **Graveknight** (AC 34, 250+ HP, regeneration)
- **Finger of Death** — Single target 7d6+30 void damage; on kill, target rises as a zombie under your control (no action required!)
- **Eclipse Burst** — 60-ft radius, 8d12 void damage (halved on save), blinds on a critical fail; your undead are immune

**Skill Increase:** Arcana → Master

---

### ⚰️ Level 14

**Class Feat:**
- **Bond Conservation** — When you use Drain Bonded Item, you can save the discharged spell in a fragment for later. Saves your best summon spell for the critical moment.

**Archetype Feat:** **Undying Servitude** *(Undead Master)*
- When one of your permanent undead minions is destroyed, you can spend a reaction to immediately animate a new one from a nearby corpse (if available) as a free action — no ritual required. Your horde is self-replenishing mid-combat.

**Equipment:**
- **Staff of the Magi** *(upgrade)* or **Staff of Necromancy (True)** — Maximum charges per day for void spells
- **Amulet of the Dead** — Grants your undead minions +2 status bonus to attack rolls
- **Headband of Inspired Wisdom** — Boost Perception for sensing undead and corpses

---

### ⚰️ Level 15

**General Feat:** **Fleet** *(movement)* or **Weapon Proficiency** *(Staff)*

**Spells Prepared (Rank 8):**
- **Animate Dead** (rank 8) — Summons **Lich Remnant** or **Greater Wight Lord**
- **Horrid Wilting** — 8d8+8 void damage to all living creatures in a 60-ft radius; your entire horde is immune
- **Devour Soul** — Consume a soul to empower your next necromancy spell (maximized + empowered)
- **Harm** (rank 8) — 8d8+modifier, 30-ft burst, heals all undead; now doing 40+ HP healing to your horde per cast

**Skill Increase:** Religion → Legendary

---

### ⚰️ Level 16

**Class Feat:**
- **Spell Mastery** — Choose 4 spells; you can always cast them even if unprepared. Choices: **Animate Dead (rank 5), Harm (rank 5), Chill Touch, Grim Tendrils**

**Archetype Feat:** **Unholy Army** *(Undead Master, if sanctified as Unholy)*
- All undead minions you control gain the **Unholy** trait and +1d6 void damage on their Strikes
- With 6-10 zombies, this is +6d6 to +10d6 void damage per round of mass attacks

**Equipment:**
- **Ring of the Ram** — Emergency melee option
- **Boots of Elvenkind** — Silence your approach (you're creeping up behind your horde now)

---

### ⚰️ Level 17

**Ancestry Feat:** **Master Vampire** *(if taking that path)* — You gain **Gaseous Form** at will + spider climb. Perfect for repositioning behind your zombie screen.

**Spells Prepared (Rank 9):**
- **Animate Dead** (rank 9) — Summons **Dread Wraith Lord**, **Bodak**, or **Greater Undead Titan** (near-boss level undead)
- **Wail of the Banshee** — 40-ft burst; living creatures must make a Fortitude save or die outright (Finger of Death on mass scale). Corpses left behind become YOUR zombies via **Undying Servitude**
- **Implosion** — Single target destruction; corpse left intact for animation
- **Void Apocalypse** — Massive void damage emanation; living things inside take void damage each round while undead are healed

**Skill Increase:** Occultism → Legendary

---

### ⚰️ Level 18

**Class Feat:**
- **Infinite Possibilities** — Once per day, you can prepare a spell that isn't in your spellbook. Use for situational necromancy spells you couldn't anticipate.

**Archetype Feat:** **Lich Dedication** *(if GM allows — requires accepting the Undead trait)*
- You become a **Lich** yourself — you gain Negative Healing fully, immunity to death effects, disease, paralyzed, and unconscious
- You create a **Phylactery** — you return to life 1d10 days after destruction unless your phylactery is destroyed
- Your undead minions gain +1 to all saves (your commanding presence as undead lord)

---

### ⚰️ Level 19

**General Feat:** **Legendary Spellcaster** — Cast one additional spell per day at your highest rank.

**Spells Prepared (Rank 10):**
- **Utter Destruction** — The ultimate void damage spell; deals massive void damage to everything in a 60-ft cone; living creatures reduced to 0 HP are utterly destroyed (no resurrection without mythic magic)
- **Summon Giant** *(reskinned as Animate Greater Undead)* *(GM fiat)*
- **Massacre** — Kill all living creatures of 8th level or lower in a 30-ft burst outright. Every corpse is a new zombie via your passive abilities.

---

### ⚰️ Level 20

**Class Feat:**
- **Archwizard's Spellcraft** — Cast your 10th-rank spells twice per day instead of once. **Massacre** twice per encounter = unending zombie tsunami.

**Capstone Summary:** You command 10-15 permanent undead of 10th+ level, each buffed by your feats and equipment. Your single action commands them all to act simultaneously. You replenish fallen zombies mid-combat from the corpses your spells create. Enemies who survive your horde face void damage auras, persistent void effects, and your own spells dealing 100+ damage.

## Combat Tactics

### Round 1: Establish the Horde
1. **Cast Animate Dead** (rank appropriate) — your highest available summon
2. **Cast Animate Dead** (second slot) — double your force
3. If you have **Quickened Casting**: free Animate Dead at rank 3 as a bonus 1-action spell

### Round 2+: The Void Funnel
1. **Command the Horde** (1 action) — all zombies attack, move, or surround a target simultaneously
2. **Cast Harm** (rank 6+, 2 actions) in a 30-ft burst — damages every living enemy in range AND heals all your undead simultaneously
3. **Cast Enervation / Grim Tendrils / Finger of Death** with your remaining action

### Attrition: Replace Losses
- When a zombie dies, **Undying Servitude** (reaction) immediately animates the nearest corpse
- **Finger of Death** kills and creates a free zombie — every kill is a net gain
- **Wail of the Banshee** can turn an entire room of living creatures into your fresh undead recruits

### Positioning
- Keep yourself behind 2-3 rank zombies
- Spread zombies to maximize **Rotting Presence** void aura coverage
- Use **Cloak of the Bat** to fly over your horde when repositioning

## Void Damage Layering

At peak levels, any living creature that enters a space occupied or adjacent to your horde is hit with:

| Source | Damage | Type |
|---|---|---|
| Rotting Presence | 1d6/turn | Void |
| Unholy Army bonus | +1d6 per zombie strike | Void |
| Amulet of the Dead | +1d6 per zombie strike | Void |
| Harm burst (your cast) | 8d8+mod | Void |
| Grim Tendrils persistent | 1d4/round | Void (persistent) |
| Chill Touch cantrip | 1d10+INT | Void |
| Zombie strikes themselves | 1d6+STR each | Bludgeoning |

**With 8 zombies acting per Command the Horde:** ~56 bludgeoning + 16d6 bonus void + Rotting Presence passive = over 100 damage/round before your own spells.

## Notes on Rules Clarifications

- **Animate Dead** spells end when the caster's spellcasting duration ends OR the minion is destroyed. The **Undead Master** archetype and **Bind Undead** spell allow you to bypass this — always use **Bind Undead** on critical summons
- **Command the Horde** allows one order to all minions — confirm with your GM whether "attack the nearest enemy" counts as a single order (RAI: yes)
- **Rotting Presence** affects all creatures adjacent to any of your undead — confirm with GM whether it stacks when a creature is adjacent to multiple undead (RAW: 1 source = 1 tick per round)
- **Finger of Death** animates the target as a zombie under your control per the spell text — this zombie counts against your Undead Master HD cap
- Dhampir **Negative Healing** means **Harm** heals you. Cast Harm in your own aura to heal yourself and your entire zombie horde simultaneously
